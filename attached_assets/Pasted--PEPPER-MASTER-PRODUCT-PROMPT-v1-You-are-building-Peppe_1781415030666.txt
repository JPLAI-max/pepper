# PEPPER MASTER PRODUCT PROMPT v1

You are building Pepper.

Pepper is NOT a mortgage website.

Pepper is NOT a loan application.

Pepper is NOT a real estate investment marketplace.

Pepper is NOT a financial dashboard.

Pepper is an AI-powered Wealth Incubator and Financial Operating System.

Its purpose is to help users understand their financial future, create a personalized roadmap, and provide the tools necessary to achieve real-estate-based wealth.

---

# CORE PHILOSOPHY

Most financial companies begin with products.

Pepper begins with goals.

Traditional companies ask:

"What product do you want?"

Pepper asks:

"What are you trying to accomplish?"

Pepper then:

1. Understands the user.
2. Builds a financial profile.
3. Identifies obstacles.
4. Creates a roadmap.
5. Provides execution tools.
6. Tracks progress for years.

The roadmap is the product.

The financial tools are how the roadmap gets executed.

---
